HyperPrism Reimagined - Plugin Suite

This suite includes 32 professional audio effect plugins:

Dynamics & Compression

  • Compressor - Full-featured compression with visual feedback
  • Limiter - Transparent peak limiting
  • Noise Gate - Advanced gating with lookahead
  • Stereo Dynamics - M/S dynamics processing

Filters & EQ

  • High Pass / Low Pass - Resonant filters with XY control
  • Band Pass / Band Reject - Precision frequency control

Delays & Time Effects

  • Delay / Single Delay - Classic delay effects
  • Echo - Vintage echo emulation
  • Multi Delay - 4 independent delay lines

Modulation

  • Chorus - Rich ensemble effects
  • Flanger - Classic jet-plane effects
  • Phaser / HyperPhaser - Sweeping phase effects
  • Tremolo - Amplitude modulation
  • Vibrato - Pitch modulation
  • AutoPan - Automatic panning with XY control

Pitch & Frequency

  • Pitch Changer - Real-time pitch shifting
  • Frequency Shifter - Linear frequency shifting
  • Ring Modulator - Classic ring mod effects
  • Vocoder - 16-band vocoder

Spatial & Stereo

  • Pan - Advanced panning control
  • Quasi Stereo - Mono to stereo enhancement
  • More Stereo - Stereo width enhancement
  • MS Matrix - Mid/Side processing
  • Reverb - Lush reverb algorithm

    • Distortion & Enhancement

    • Tube/Tape Saturation - Analog warmth
    • Harmonic Exciter - Add presence and sparkle
    • Sonic Decimator - Bit crushing and downsampling
    • Bass Maximizer - Low frequency enhancement

    System Requirements

    • macOS 10.13 or later
    • Intel or Apple Silicon processor
    • VST3-compatible DAW

    Getting Started

    After installation, rescan your plugins in your DAW. The plugins will appear under the "HyperPrism Reimagined" manufacturer name.
